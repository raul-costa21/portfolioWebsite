About
Me

After become unemployed in 2020 I decided to use the spare time I had to learn how to code. I choose this field because since I was a kid I had (and have) an interest and skill to everything related to computers. In the beginning I didn't know which path to choose since the programing area has many options. I was advised by a senior developer to try web development and so far I am loving it! For me, web development is a combination of style and the design with logic and problem solving.
